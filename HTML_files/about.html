About Spotify

Spotify is a digital music, podcast, and video streaming service that gives you access to millions of songs and other content from creators all over the world. Founded in 2006, Spotify has transformed the way people listen to music, giving them the ability to discover new artists, genres, and songs through a wide range of personalized playlists and curated collections.
